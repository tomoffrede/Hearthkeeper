About The Hearthkeeper

The Hearthkeeper is a personal project by Tom Offrede — a free, open web app that turns the never-ending work of keeping a home into a light RPG adventure.

The idea is simple: house cleaning is repetitive, unglamorous, and easy to put off. Gamification doesn't make it glamorous, but it can make it feel a little more purposeful — and a little more rewarding. Complete a quest, earn XP, level up your character, unlock your keep, read a story that unfolds only because you did the work.

The app is entirely free, has no ads, and collects no data beyond what is needed to save your progress. It is built with Firebase for authentication and data storage, and uses the Grok AI API to score custom quests.

This is a hobby project, offered freely to anyone who wants it. No commercial interest. No tracking. Just a clean house and a good story.

Data Privacy · Datenschutzerklärung

English

What data is collected: When you register, your chosen username (stored internally as a pseudo-email) and a hashed password are stored by Firebase Authentication (Google). Your game progress — character, XP, quest history — is stored in Firebase Firestore. No real name, email address, or personal information is required or stored.

Why: Solely to save your game progress and allow sign-in from any device.

Who has access: Only you. Data is protected by Firebase security rules that prevent any other user from reading or writing your data.

Third-party services: Firebase (Google LLC, USA) is used for authentication and storage. When you submit a custom quest, its name is sent to the Grok API (xAI) to determine an XP value. No personal data is included in these requests.

Deletion: You can delete your account at any time from the Settings tab, permanently removing all your data.

Deutsch

Welche Daten werden erhoben: Bei der Registrierung werden Ihr Benutzername (intern als Pseudo-E-Mail gespeichert) und ein verschlüsseltes Passwort durch Firebase Authentication (Google) gespeichert. Ihr Spielfortschritt wird in Firebase Firestore gespeichert. Es werden keine echten Namen oder E-Mail-Adressen gespeichert.

Zweck: Ausschließlich zur Speicherung Ihres Spielfortschritts und zur geräteübergreifenden Anmeldung.

Zugriff: Nur Sie haben Zugriff. Die Daten sind durch Firebase-Sicherheitsregeln geschützt.

Drittanbieter: Firebase (Google LLC, USA) für Authentifizierung und Speicherung. Beim Einreichen einer eigenen Aufgabe wird deren Name an die Grok API (xAI) gesendet. Keine personenbezogenen Daten werden dabei übermittelt.

Löschung: Sie können Ihr Konto jederzeit im Einstellungen-Tab löschen.
